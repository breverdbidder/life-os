## ðŸ“‹ TIER 1: Direct Outreach (0-10 Users)

**Timeline:** Week 1-2  
**Goal:** Get first 10 users who will give honest feedback  
**Budget:** $0  

### Method 1: Personal Network (Days 1-3)

**Your List:**
- [ ] Former colleagues (10-20 people)
- [ ] University friends (10-20 people)
- [ ] Industry contacts (10-20 people)
- [ ] Friends/family (only if ICP) (5-10 people)

**Outreach Template:**
```
Subject: Quick favor - trying something new

Hey [Name],

I'm building [one-sentence pitch] and would love your 
brutal honesty on whether it solves a real problem.

Not asking you to buy anything - just 15 min to:
1. Show you what I've built
2. Watch you try to use it
3. Get your unfiltered feedback

Free [your product] access forever if you help me.

Available this week?

[Your name]
```

**Success Metrics:**
- 30% response rate (6 of 20)
- 50% convert to user (3 of 6)
- **Expected: 5-10 users from network**

### Method 2: LinkedIn/Twitter DMs (Days 4-7)

**Target:** People who match your ICP exactly

**Finding Them:**
- LinkedIn: Search "[role] at [company type]"
- Twitter: Search "[problem] frustrating" or "[current solution] sucks"
- Look for people complaining about your problem

**Outreach Template:**
```
Hi [Name],

Saw your post about [problem]. I'm building [solution]
specifically for [their situation].

Would love to show you a quick demo (5 min) and get 
your take on whether this actually helps or is useless.

No sales pitch - genuinely just validating if I'm 
building something useful.

[Link to calendar]
```

**Volume:**
- Send 50 DMs
- 10-20% response rate (5-10 responses)
- 50% convert to demo (3-5 demos)
- **Expected: 2-5 users from cold outreach**

### Method 3: Relevant Communities (Days 8-14)

**Find Your Communities:**
- Slack workspaces (product-specific)
- Discord servers (industry-specific)
- Reddit subreddits (problem-specific)
- Facebook groups (demographic-specific)

**Rules:**
1. Join and lurk for 2-3 days
2. Provide value first (answer questions)
3. Only share when directly relevant
4. Frame as "built this for myself, anyone else need it?"

**Community Share Template:**

### Method 2: Community Engagement (Daily)

**Afternoon (20 min):**

- Mention product only when directly relevant
- Be genuinely helpful (not sleazy)

**Expected: 30-50 users from communities (month 2-3)**

## ðŸ“‹ TIER 3: Scalable Channels (100-1000+ Users)

**Timeline:** Month 4-12  
**Goal:** Build repeatable acquisition systems  
**Budget:** $500-5,000/month  

### Method 1: SEO Content Engine

**The System:** Publish consistently, rank gradually, compound traffic

**Keyword Research:**
- Use Ahrefs/SEMrush/Ubersuggest
- Target: 300-3,000 searches/month
- Difficulty: <30 (low competition)
- Intent: Problem-aware or solution-aware

**Content Production:**
- Hire writer: $100-300 per post (1500-2500 words)
- Or write yourself: 4-6 hours per post
- Volume: 8-12 posts/month
- Interlink related posts

**SEO Checklist per Post:**
- [ ] Keyword in title, H1, first paragraph
- [ ] 3-5 internal links to other posts
- [ ] 2-3 external links to authority sites
- [ ] Meta description (155 chars)
- [ ] Alt text on all images
- [ ] URL slug matches keyword

**Expected: 200-500 users/month from SEO (month 6-12)**

### Method 2: Paid Advertising (When CAC < LTV/3)

**Don't start paid until:**
- [ ] You know your LTV ($X per customer)
- [ ] You have conversion rate data
- [ ] You can afford to test ($1K-2K)

**Google Ads Strategy:**
```
Start: $30/day ($900/month)
Target: Bottom-of-funnel keywords
Example: "[competitor] alternative"
         "best [solution] for [use case]"
         "[specific problem] software"

Expected: $20-50 CPA (cost per acquisition)
Goal: CPA < LTV/3
```

**Facebook/LinkedIn Ads Strategy:**
```
Start: $20/day ($600/month)
Target: Job titles + interests
Creative: Problem-focused (not product)
Format: Video > Carousel > Image

Expected: $30-70 CPA
Goal: Test audiences, find winners
```

**Testing Approach:**
- Week 1: Test 5 audiences ($20 each = $100)
- Week 2: Double down on best 2 ($300 total)
- Week 3-4: Optimize best performer ($500 total)
- Month 2: Scale winner or pivot

**Expected: 50-200 users/month from ads (month 6-12)**

**Timing:** When you have social proof (50+ users, testimonials)

## ðŸš¨ Common Distribution Mistakes

### Mistake 1: Paid Ads Too Early

**Don't run ads until:**
- You know your LTV
- You have product-market fit
- You can afford to burn $2K testing

**Why:** Ads are expensive, you'll waste money acquiring users who churn

### Mistake 2: SEO Without Search Volume

**Don't write content that:**
- Has <100 searches/month
- Targets the wrong intent (informational vs commercial)
- You can't rank for (too competitive)

**Do:** Keyword research FIRST, then write

### Mistake 3: Trying Everything at Once

**Don't:**
- Post on 10 platforms daily
- Run 5 ad campaigns simultaneously
- Write 20 blog posts/month

**Do:** Pick 1-2 channels, master them, then add more

### Mistake 4: No Clear CTA

**Every piece of content needs:**
- One clear call-to-action
- Easy next step
- Compelling reason to act now

### Mistake 5: Ignoring Retention

**Acquisition doesn't matter if:**
- Users don't activate (complete onboarding)
- Users don't return (retention <30%)
- Users churn immediately (bad product)

**Fix the product before scaling distribution**
