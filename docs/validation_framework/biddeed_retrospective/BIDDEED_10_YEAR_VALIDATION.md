   - Opportunity: Miss 5-10 deals/year while researching

   - Morning: Check RealForeclose for upcoming auctions
   - Week Before: Drive to courthouse, pull records (4 hours)
   - Day Before: Call attorney with lien questions
   - Auction Day: Make gut-feeling bids based on incomplete data

   - "Show me you caught a lien I would have missed" ‚Üê Demonstrated 10+ times
