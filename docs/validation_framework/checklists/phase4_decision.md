## Decision Matrix

### GREEN (Build MVP) ✅
**Criteria:**
- 4/4 quantitative thresholds met
- 3+ qualitative strong signals
- <2 warning signals
- Clear monetization path identified

**Action:** Proceed to Phase 5 (Pre-Build Preparation)

---

### YELLOW (Pivot) ⚠️
**Criteria:**
- 2-3/4 quantitative thresholds met
- Some strong signals present
- Major objection pattern identified

**Action:** 
1. Reframe positioning based on interviews
2. Update landing page messaging
3. Run 1-week retest (100 new visitors)
4. If conversion improves → GREEN
5. If no improvement → RED

---

### RED (Kill Idea) ❌
**Criteria:**
- <2/4 quantitative thresholds met
- No strong qualitative signals
- 2+ warning signals
- Market too small (<$10M TAM)

**Action:**
1. Document learnings (what killed it?)
2. Archive all materials
3. Move to next idea
4. **This is a WIN** - you learned before building
