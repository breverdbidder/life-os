## Day 38: Early Adopter Pipeline

### Segment Your Waitlist (from Phase 3)

**Hot Leads (10%)** - Clicked Primary CTA + engaged in interview
- Action: Schedule 1:1 demos this week
- Offer: Design partner program (free beta â†’ paid)

**Warm Leads (30%)** - Clicked Secondary CTA or moderate engagement  
- Action: Nurture email sequence (3 emails over 2 weeks)
- Content: Problem education, demo snippets

**Cold Leads (60%)** - Clicked Tertiary CTA only
- Action: Educational content drip (1 email/week)
- Goal: Move to warm over 30 days

## Day 39-40: Beta Program Design

### Target: 10-20 Design Partners
- **Why 10-20?** Manageable feedback loop, enough for patterns
- **Pricing:** Free beta â†’ commit to paid conversion
- **Duration:** 6-8 weeks
- **Support:** Founder-led (you do everything)

### Weekly Check-In Questions
1. "What did you try to accomplish this week?"
2. "What worked? What didn't?"
3. "If you could change ONE thing, what would it be?"
4. "On a scale 1-10, how likely are you to pay $X/month after beta?"

### Beta Feedback Framework
- **Week 1:** Onboarding experience
- **Week 3:** Core workflow usability
- **Week 6:** Feature requests + pricing discussion
- **Week 8:** Go/no-go on paid conversion
