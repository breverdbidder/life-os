## Diet Protocol

### Monday-Thursday (Strict Keto)
- Net carbs: <20g/day
- Protein: 1g per lb body weight
- Fat: 60-70% of calories
- Focus: Athletic performance, mental clarity

### Friday-Sunday (Moderate Carbs for Shabbat)
- Net carbs: <50g/day
- Allow small portions of rice/couscous for Shabbat meals
- Maintain kosher observance
- Still keto-adapted

## Kosher Requirements

✅ **Meat:** Kosher certified beef, chicken, lamb only  
✅ **Dairy:** Separate from meat meals (6 hour wait)  
✅ **Pareve:** Neutral items (vegetables, oils, nuts)  
❌ **Never:** Pork, shellfish, mixing meat/dairy

## Keto Staples (Kosher)

### Fats & Oils
- Olive oil (extra virgin)
- Avocado oil
- Coconut oil
- Schmaltz (chicken fat)
- Beef tallow

### Proteins
- Beef (80/20 ground, ribeye, chuck roast)
- Chicken (thighs, wings, dark meat)
- Fish (salmon, tuna, sardines)
- Eggs (pasture-raised)

### Low-Carb Vegetables
- Leafy greens (spinach, kale, romaine)
- Cruciferous (broccoli, cauliflower, brussels sprouts)
- Zucchini, eggplant, peppers
- Celery, cucumber, radishes

### Keto Baking/Coating
- Almond flour
- Coconut flour
- Crushed nuts (pecans, walnuts)
- Flax meal
