# Lead Research Assistant

Identifies potential customers, qualifies them, provides outreach strategies.

## When to Use
- Finding first customers
- Expanding to new segments
- Building sales pipeline
- Enterprise acquisition

## Process

1. **Analyze Product**: Understand what you're selling, ICP
2. **Research Companies**: Find matches using:
   - Y Combinator portfolios
   - Industry-specific tool users
   - Geographic/size filters
   - Technology stack indicators
3. **Qualify Leads**: Score 1-10 based on:
   - Funding/revenue fit
   - Tech stack alignment
   - Growth indicators
   - Decision maker accessibility
4. **Provide Intel**:
   - Decision maker + LinkedIn
   - Company context
   - Conversation starters
   - Pain points

## Output Format (per lead)

```
**Company Name**
Priority Score: 8/10
Location: Melbourne, FL
Size: 50 employees
Funding: $5M Series A
Why Good Fit: Uses PropTech, expanding to FL
Decision Maker: John Smith, VP Sales
LinkedIn: linkedin.com/in/johnsmith
Outreach: "Noticed you're expanding FL operations. 
BidDeed helped similar firms cut acquisition costs 40%..."
```

## Best Practices
- Research 2-3x more than requested, filter to best
- Verify decision makers via LinkedIn
- Include specific pain points
- Provide warm intro angles
- Focus on growth/hiring signals

## Example Usage
```
"Look at BidDeed.AI and find 10 real estate investors in Brevard County.
Use lead research assistant."
```
