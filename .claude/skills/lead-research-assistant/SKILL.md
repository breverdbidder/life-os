# Lead Research Assistant

Identifies potential customers + provides outreach intel.

## Process
1. Analyze your product/ICP
2. Research companies matching criteria
3. Score leads 1-10 priority
4. Provide decision maker + LinkedIn
5. Generate conversation starters

## Output Per Lead
- Company name
- Priority score (1-10)
- Location, size, funding
- Why good fit
- Decision maker: Name + Title + LinkedIn
- Outreach approach with talking points

## Usage
```
"Look at BidDeed.AI and find 10 real estate investors in Brevard County.
Use lead research assistant."
```
