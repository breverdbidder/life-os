# Swim Venues Reference - Michael Shapira

## Florida Competitive Pools

### FAST - Florida Aquatics Swimming & Training Center
- **Location:** Ocala, FL
- **Pool Speed:** FAST ⚡
- **Facilities:**
  - Indoor: 10-lane 50m/25yd pool
  - Outdoor: 8-lane pool
  - Dryland: 2,475 sq ft fitness center
- **Preferred Hotel:** Staybridge Suites Ocala
  - Phone: (352) 619-8080
  - Distance: 8 miles
- **Drive from Satellite Beach:** ~2.5 hours (142 miles)
- **Parking:** Ample, free
- **Notes:** Known for fast times, excellent competition venue. Indoor pool ideal for year-round training.

### Satellite High School Pool
- **Location:** Satellite Beach, FL
- **Pool Speed:** Standard
- **Pool Type:** 25y
- **Notes:** Home pool, practice facility

---

## Pool Speed Guide
- **FAST** ⚡ - Deep water, good gutters, minimal turbulence, produces fast times
- **STANDARD** - Normal competition pool
- **SLOW** - Shallow, older design, more turbulence
