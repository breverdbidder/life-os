## Review Ready Email (T-1)

**Subject:** Tomorrow is the BIG day! Here's your review prep guide

---

Hi [NAME],

**Tomorrow, [BOOK TITLE] launches on Amazon!**

I'll send you the direct review link in the morning, but here's how to prepare:

**Writing your review (takes 5 min):**
1. Start with your overall impression (1-2 sentences)
2. Mention 1-2 specific things you liked
3. Say who would benefit from this book
4. Give your recommendation

**Example structure:**
> "I found [BOOK TITLE] to be [overall impression]. I especially appreciated [specific element]. This book is perfect for [target reader]. Highly recommended!"

**Important notes:**
- Be honest (Amazon detects fake reviews)
- Don't mention you received a free copy
- Reviews under 20 words get filtered

See you tomorrow!
[AUTHOR NAME]
